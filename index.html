My name is Anna.

Until now, I only saw princesses in movies. Those beautiful, mysterious women draped in gold. I never thought I would meet one — and that she would change my life forever.

I was invited to Doha for a private beauty conference. At a reception, I noticed a woman whose presence commanded the entire room. Her skin was luminous — porcelain-smooth with an inner glow that no makeup could replicate. I assumed she was in her thirties. She was approaching sixty.

She was a princess from one of the ruling families. That evening she shared her secret — ancient beauty rituals using plant-based peptides, formulas her family had refined over centuries. They never used animal-derived ingredients. Everything came from plants, seeds, and desert botanicals.

“I looked at her and couldn’t believe my eyes. Her skin was flawless — not in the way of someone who has had work done, but in a way that spoke of deep, natural radiance. She was nearly sixty, yet she glowed like a woman half her age.”
— Anna

I returned home with notebooks full of her formulas and sat down in my father’s laboratory. Together, we translated her ancient wisdom into modern science — combining cutting-edge vegan peptides like Neodermyl and Argirelina with traditional botanical extracts.

The result was Peptidermax and its revolutionary IN Technology — Instead of Needle. A cream that delivers what only injections could promise, powered entirely by plants. Clinical tests confirmed it: visible wrinkle reduction in 28 days.
